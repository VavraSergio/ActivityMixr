ActivityMixr Help

Welcome to the ActivityMixr Help Center! Here's a quick guide on how to use ActivityMixr:

How to Use ActivityMixr

  1. Visit the ActivityMixr website at www.activitymixr.com.
  2. On the homepage, you'll find a button to help you get started. Click on 'Generate' to start.
  3. When you're on the generate page, enter in some basic information on the kind of activity you'd like to do.
  4. Click the generate button and boom! An activity is generated right before your eyes!
  5. A playlist is then generated based off of that activity! You can see playlists added to the 'playlist' page.
  6. Thats all there is to it! Ready to get started? Just click here!
